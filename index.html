About

We​ ​are​ ​pleased​ ​to​ ​announce​ ​Missouri​ ​S&T​ ​will​ ​host​ ​its​ ​first​ ​Major​ ​League​ ​Hackathon, ShamHacks,​ ​February​ ​9th​ ​through​ ​February​ ​11th.​ ​Rather​ ​than​ ​focus​ ​on​ ​a​ ​broad​ ​technological theme​ ​or​ ​piece​ ​of​ ​technology,​ ​ShamHacks​ ​aims​ ​to​ ​make​ ​positive​ ​contributions​ ​to​ ​society.​ ​With Rolla’s​ ​close​ ​proximity​ ​to​ ​Fort​ ​Leonard​ ​Wood​ ​and​ ​the​ ​emergence​ ​of​ ​veteran​ ​affairs​ ​in​ ​the mainstream,​ ​our​ ​theme​ ​will​ ​involve​ ​helping​ ​our​ ​U.S.​ ​military​ ​veterans​ ​and​ ​active​ ​duty​ ​service members. The​ ​event​ ​will​ ​draw​ ​the​ ​brightest​ ​high​ ​school​ ​and​ ​university​ ​students​ ​from​ ​Missouri​ ​and its​ ​surrounding​ ​states​ ​to​ ​code​ ​for​ ​the​ ​cause​ ​for​ ​40​ ​hours​ ​straight.​ ​ShamHacks​ ​will​ ​culminate​ ​with presentations​ ​of​ ​the​ ​best​ ​hacks​ ​and​ ​reward​ ​those​ ​who​ ​best​ ​solve​ ​the​ ​3​ ​problem​ ​statements released​ ​at​ ​the​ ​start​ ​of​ ​the​ ​competition.

ShamHacks will take over the Havener Student Center on campus for the event. The student center has ample room to accomodate hackers, sleepers, and corporate recruiters. Tables, Chairs, powerstrips, and free Wi-fi are provided. Feel free to sleep in the building as well!

A late night snack will be provided Friday, February 9 after the opening ceremony. Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and a late night snack will be provided on February 10. Breakfast is provided on Sunday, February 11th while judging takes place. All meals will be held in the Curtis Laws Wilson Library.

The event is organized and run by the Missouri S&T ACM Student Chapter in conjunction with the Curtis Laws Wilson Library, ICORPS, COER, and Student Council.
